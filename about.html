Get Ready For Thrill!

One of the coolest features in the room is the airbag, which isn’t just your average airbag.
It’s got the very latest technology developed to mimic a real landing ramp, but with the benefits of airbag impact absorption if anything goes wrong.
Basically – you can practice your new trick into an airbag and if you land it, you can ride away. “It’s like landing in powder”, co-owner Joakim Kumlin explains.
It could be the most revolutionary thing to hit freestyle action sport in years.
To make sure all extreme sport junkies get to reap the benefits of this, The Dome has created specially designed skis and snowboards with wheels so that snow sport people can practice their tricks together with the bikers, skaters and kickbikers.
